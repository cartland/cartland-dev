Here are some of my favorite projects, ranging from software development to sustainable technology and interactive art. I'm passionate about building things that are both useful and innovative, and I enjoy exploring new technologies that push the boundary of my creative skills.

Android Build Time

I host "Android Build Time," a video podcast series produced by the Android team at Google. I interview developers about apps and technologies relevant to professional Android developers.

World Solar Challenge

As team lead for CalSol, the UC Berkeley Solar Vehicle Team, I led the development of a solar-powered car for the 2011 Veolia World Solar Challenge. This involved managing mechanical, electrical, and business teams to secure funding and build a street-legal vehicle from scratch, which we then raced across the continent of Australia.

Smart Garage Door

This project involved designing and building a smart garage door system with internet-connected microcontrollers and a companion Android app. The embedded software manages sensors and actuators to control the garage door, while the Android app provides a user interface with features like push notifications for door status and remote control.

GitHub

Marauder's Map

I built an interactive Marauder's Map for a Halloween party. The animations are drawn with an HTML5 Canvas runs in a WebView in an Android app. The app was deployed on phones, tablets, and Android TV. We added a bit of magic with NFC magic wands that allowed the wizard to wave their wand and see their footsteps appear on the map in their real location.
