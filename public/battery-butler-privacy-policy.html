Battery Butler Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18th, 2026

Important Note: This app is designed for private use by the author (Christopher Cartland) and individuals specifically granted access. If you have come across this app accidentally, please delete it.

You can find the app on the Google Play Store here: Battery Butler on Google Play. This link should only be accessed by those who have been given express permission by the author.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Usage data: How often the app is opened, the version of the app, how often data is delivered, and other metrics to ensure the app is working.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Monitor and improve app performance: Analyze usage data to identify bugs, optimize features, and enhance user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties.

Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of your Battery Butler account and associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to chris@chriscart.land with the subject line "Battery Butler Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address associated with your Battery Butler account.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been processed.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your account information
  • Usage data associated with your account

Data that may be retained

  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked back to your account.

Account deletion requests will be processed within 30 days. You may also uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data from your device.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Data is stored using Google and Amazon infrastructure with proper security practices in place. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us
