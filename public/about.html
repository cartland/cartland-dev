Software Engineer and Climate Advocate

As a software engineer with 12 years of experience at Google, I've had the opportunity to contribute to impactful projects like Android TV, which now reaches over 270 million monthly active devices. My focus has been on developer APIs that enable professionals to create innovative Android experiences.

Beyond my work at Google, I'm driven by a passion for sustainable technology. In college, I led a team that built a solar-powered car for the World Solar Challenge 2011, where we raced a street-legal vehicle powered only by the sun across Australia.

I continue to explore this passion for software by building projects in my free time, such as a smart garage door with Internet-connected microcontrollers and a companion Android app.
