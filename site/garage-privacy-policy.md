# Garage Privacy Policy

_Effective Date: November 30th, 2024_

**Important Note:** This app is designed for private use by the author (Christopher Cartland) and individuals specifically granted access. If you have come across this app accidentally, please delete it.

You can find the app on the Google Play Store here: [Garage on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.chriscartland.garage). This link should only be accessed by those who have been given express permission by the author.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- Usage data: How often the app is opened, the version of the app, how often data is delivered, and other metrics to ensure the app is working.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Monitor and improve app performance: Analyze usage data to identify bugs, optimize features, and enhance user experience.

## Data Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties.

## Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your data:

- Delete your app: This will remove all stored data from your device.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Data is stored using Firebase and Google infrastructure with proper security practices in place. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children.

## Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
